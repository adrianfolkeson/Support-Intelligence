# Email Outreach Templates

## Quick Stats
- **Target:** 5 customers in 90 days
- **Daily outreach:** 10-15 emails/day
- **Expected response rate:** 5-10%
- **Demo booking rate:** 30-50% of responders
- **Close rate:** 20-30% of demos

## Template 1: Cold Email (Zendesk Users)

**Subject:** AI for {Company} support tickets?

```
Hi {FirstName},

I noticed {Company} uses Zendesk - we built an AI tool that analyzes support tickets to find patterns and recommendations.

What it does:
• Identifies common customer issues automatically
• Predicts churn risk for each customer
• Generates weekly insight reports
• Saves 5-10 hours per week per support agent

We're at $249/mo with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

Would you be open to a 10-minute demo this week?

Best,
{Your Name}
Founder, Support Intelligence
```

## Outreach Sequence

### Week 1: Cold Outreach
- Day 1: Send 15 emails (Template 1)
- Day 2: Send 15 emails + 10 LinkedIn (Template 2)
- Day 3: Send 15 emails
- Day 4: Send 15 emails + 10 LinkedIn

## Personalization Tips

1. **Always use their name**
2. **Reference their company specifically**
3. **Mention something recent about their company** (news, product launch, etc.)
4. **Keep emails under 150 words**
5. **Ask one specific question**
6. **End with a clear call-to-action**

---

## Common Objections & Responses

### "Too expensive"
> "I understand budget is important. Many of our customers found that the time savings alone (5-10 hours/week) pays for the tool. Would a 30-day money-back guarantee help reduce the risk?"

### "Not the right time"
> "Totally understand. When would be a better time to reconnect? Next month? I'll follow up then."

### "We already have similar tools"
> "That's great! What do you currently use? I'm curious if our AI analysis adds anything different."

### "Send me more info"
> "Absolutely! Here's a quick video: [link]. Let me know if you have any questions after watching."

---

## Best Practices

1. **Send emails Tue-Thu mornings** (best open rates)
2. **Personalize the first line** (not just the greeting)
3. **Keep subject lines under 50 characters**
4. **Use plain text emails** (no HTML templates)
5. **Include your real email signature**
6. **Test different send times**
7. **Track everything in a spreadsheet**
