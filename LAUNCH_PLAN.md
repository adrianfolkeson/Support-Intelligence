### 2.3 Email Templates

#### Template A: Cold Outreach (Zendesk Users)

```
Subject: AI for {Company} support tickets?

Hi {Name},

I noticed {Company} uses Zendesk - we built an AI tool that analyzes support tickets to find patterns and recommendations.

What it does:
• Identifies common customer issues
• Suggests response templates
• Generates weekly insight reports
• Estimates saving 5-10 hours/week

We're at $249/mo with a 14-day free trial.

Would you be open to a 10-minute demo this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### Template B: Warm Introduction

```
Subject: Quick intro - AI for support teams

Hi {Name},

I wanted to introduce you to Support Intelligence - an AI tool that helps support teams work smarter.

We've helped companies like {similar company} reduce response time by 30%.

If you're open to it, I'd love to do a quick 10-min demo.

No pressure - just sharing something I think could help.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### Template C: Follow-up

```
Subject: Re: AI for support tickets

Hi {Name},

Just circling back on this - I know support teams are always busy, so no worries if now isn't the right time.

Quick question: Would it help if I sent you a 2-min video showing how it works?

Either way, best of luck with everything!

Best,
[Your Name]
```
